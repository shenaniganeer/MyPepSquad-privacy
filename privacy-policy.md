Privacy Policy for MyPepSquad

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Shenaniganeer Studios handles information in relation to the mobile application MyPepSquad (the "App").

1. Data Collection and Use

I believe in 100% user privacy. The App is designed so that your personal data remains yours.

No Developer Collection: I do not collect, store on my own servers, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information.

Subscriptions and Billing: MyPepSquad is a paid subscription service. All financial transactions are processed securely by Google Play.
I do not have access to your credit card information. I only receive transaction details (such as order IDs and subscription status) as 
required by Google to manage your access to the App.

2. Google Drive Integration (Backup & Import)

The App includes a feature that allows you to back up and import your data using your personal Google Drive account.

User-Controlled Data: Any data backed up to Google Drive is stored in a private folder within your own account.

No Access to Files: I do not have access to your Google Drive files, nor do I collect or "see" the data you choose to back up. The connection
is purely between the App on your device and your personal cloud storage.

3. Data Storage

Apart from your optional Google Drive backups, all App data is stored locally on your device. This data is not accessible by me or any third 
party. If you delete the App without backing up to your Drive, your local data will be permanently lost.

4. Children’s Privacy

MyPepSquad does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.5.
